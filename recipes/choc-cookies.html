Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

Been craving chocolate chip cookies? I got you. The craving for soft yet crunchy, chocolately cookies is a universal truth shared by everybody, no matter how old. In the following recipe, I will teach you how to realize your dreams, and if the cookies suck that is totally not my fault. Because I am the designer, the creator, and inspirer of all things delicious and amazing and fantastic tasting. I'm that guy. Good luck!

Makes 24 cookies

  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 3/4 brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 tablespoons butter, room temperature
  • 150g flour
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  1. Into a large bowl, combine your softened butter and sugar. Make sure the butter is soft enough to combine and mix. Afterwards, add in the 2 eggs and mix until just combined. Add in a dash of vanilla extract and mix again.

  2. Mix together flour and baking powder in a bowl. Combine the dry ingredients into the bowl with the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. When this is finished, fold in the chocolate chips until again the mixture is just combined. Note: It is important to mix until just combined, otherwise your cookies won't be as soft and malleable

  3. Chill dough for at least 30 minutes or leave in the freezer up to a week. When you are ready, scoop out tablespoon sized bunches of dough onto parchment paper and bake at 350 F for 8-12 minutes. Enjoy!
