Beef Tacos

Description:

These tacos are the perfect lunch/dinner idea for anybody in a quick pinch, all you need is some tortillas, ground beef, seasonings, and whatever toppings you wish and you're good to go!

Makes 12 tacos

Taco Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Optional: 1/8 teaspoon chili powder

Suggested Topping Choices:

  • Sour Cream
  • Guacamole
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Pico de Gallo
  • Mango Salsa
  • Shredded Cheese
  1. In a large bowl, combine beef with spices. Mix until thoroughly combined.
  2. TUrn stove to medium heat. Saute beef until it is orange/brown colored, then remove from heat.
  3. While the beef is cooking, warm up a tortilla by putting it in a hot pan for about 30 seconds
  4. Assemble tacos by putting ground beef on tortillas and whatever toppings you'd like
  5. ENJOY!
